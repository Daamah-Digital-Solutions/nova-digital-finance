ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

1.1 Definitions

“Default Interest” means interest charged on overdue amounts at the rate specified in Article 5.3;
“Event of Default” has the meaning set out in Article 7.1;
“Facility” means the digital financing facility granted by the Lender to the Borrower pursuant to this Agreement;
“Facility Amount” means {{ financing.bronova_amount }} PRN or its USD Equivalent as specified in Schedule A;

“Investment” means any investment made by the Borrower using the Facility on CapiMax Platforms;

“Repayment Schedule” means the schedule of installment payments set out in Schedule A;
“Settlement Currency” means United States Dollars (USD);
“USD Equivalent” means the value in United States Dollars calculated at the Platform’s prevailing exchange rate at the relevant time.

ARTICLE 2: THE FACILITY

2.1 Grant of Facility

Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Lender agrees to grant the Borrower a financing facility in the principal amount of {{ financing.bronova_amount }} PRN (the “Facility Amount”).

2.2 Purpose

The Borrower shall use the Facility exclusively for investment purposes on CapiMax Platforms as approved by the Lender. The Borrower shall not:

  • (a) Withdraw, cash out, or convert the Facility to fiat or other currencies except as expressly permitted;
  • (b) Transfer the Facility or any portion thereof to third parties;
  • (c) Use the Facility for any purpose other than investments on CapiMax Platforms;
  • (d) Engage in any speculative, illegal, or prohibited activities.

2.3 Disbursement

Upon execution of this Agreement and fulfillment of all conditions precedent, the Lender shall credit the Facility Amount to the Borrower’s platform account in the form of a Financing Instrument.

2.4 Availability Period

The Facility shall be available for utilization for a period of {{ availability_days }} days from the Disbursement Date, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

ARTICLE 3: FINANCING INSTRUMENT AND UTILIZATION

3.1 Issuance of Instrument

The Lender shall issue a Financing Instrument to the Borrower evidencing the Facility. The Financing Instrument shall be registered in the Borrower’s platform account and shall constitute prima facie evidence of the Facility.

3.2 Utilization Terms

The Borrower may utilize the Financing Instrument subject to the following conditions:

  • (a) Utilization may be in full or in partial tranches;
  • (b) Multiple investments across different CapiMax Platforms are permitted provided the aggregate does not exceed the Facility Amount;
  • (c) Each utilization shall be verified and approved by the relevant platform;
  • (d) All Investments shall be automatically subject to the Pledge created under Article 4.

3.3 Platform Integration

The Lender shall notify CapiMax Platforms of the Financing Instrument and the Pledge. The platforms shall acknowledge and record such notification in accordance with their operational procedures.

ARTICLE 4: SECURITY AND PLEDGE

4.1 Creation of Pledge

As continuing security for the payment and discharge of all amounts owing under this Agreement, the Borrower hereby pledges, charges, and assigns to the Lender all right, title, and interest in and to:

  • (a) All Investments made using the Facility on CapiMax Platforms;
  • (b) All returns, profits, dividends, and proceeds arising from such Investments;
  • (c) All rights to withdraw, transfer, or otherwise deal with such Investments.

4.2 Priority and Perfection

The Pledge shall:

  • (a) Constitute a first-ranking security interest in favor of the Lender;
  • (b) Be recorded and acknowledged by CapiMax Platforms;
  • (c) Remain in full force and effect until complete satisfaction of all obligations under this Agreement.

4.3 Restrictions on Dealing

Until release of the Pledge pursuant to Article 4.5, the Borrower shall not, without prior written consent of the Lender:

  • (a) Sell, transfer, assign, or otherwise dispose of any Investment;
  • (b) Create or permit to subsist any encumbrance over any Investment;
  • (c) Withdraw funds or proceeds from Investments except as expressly permitted.

4.4 Rights of Lender

The Lender shall have the right, upon occurrence of an Event of Default, to:

  • (a) Direct CapiMax Platforms to liquidate any or all Investments;
  • (b) Apply proceeds of liquidation toward satisfaction of the Outstanding Balance;
  • (c) Exercise any other rights available under applicable law or platform terms.

4.5 Release of Pledge

The Pledge shall be released upon:

  • (a) Full and final repayment of all amounts owing under this Agreement;
  • (b) Receipt of written confirmation from the Borrower acknowledging complete settlement;
  • (c) Issuance of a release certificate by the Lender.

ARTICLE 5: REPAYMENT TERMS

5.1 Repayment Obligation

The Borrower shall repay the Facility Amount plus Profit Share in accordance with the Repayment Schedule attached as Schedule A.

5.2 Payment Methods

The Borrower may make payments through any of the following methods:

  • (a) Digital currencies including PRN or other cryptocurrencies accepted by the Lender;
  • (b) Bank transfer in Settlement Currency to the Lender’s designated account;
  • (c) Platform wallet deduction from the Borrower’s account balance;
  • (d) Payment link provided within the Platform interface.

5.3 Default Interest

Any amount not paid when due shall bear interest at the rate of {{ default_interest_rate }}% per annum (or such other rate as may be agreed) from the due date until actual payment, calculated on a daily basis and compounded monthly.

5.4 Application of Payments

All payments received shall be applied in the following order:

  • (a) First, to costs and expenses incurred by the Lender;
  • (b) Second, to accrued and unpaid Default Interest;
  • (c) Third, to Profit Share;
  • (d) Fourth, to principal amount of the Facility.

5.5 Payment in Settlement Currency

While the Facility is denominated in PRN, all repayment calculations and collection proceedings shall be based on the USD Equivalent, ensuring accurate valuation at the time of each payment or enforcement action.

ARTICLE 6: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

6.1 Borrower’s Representations

The Borrower represents and warrants to the Lender that:

  • (a) Capacity: The Borrower has full legal capacity to enter into and perform this Agreement;
  • (b) No Conflict: The execution and performance of this Agreement does not conflict with any law, regulation, or contractual obligation binding on the Borrower;
  • (c) Accurate Information: All information provided to the Lender is true, accurate, and complete in all material respects;
  • (d) Understanding: The Borrower understands the nature and risks of digital currency financing and platform investments;
  • (e) Intended Use: The Facility will be used solely for the permitted purposes set out in Article 2.2;
  • (f) No Proceedings: There are no legal proceedings pending or threatened against the Borrower that would materially affect the ability to perform obligations hereunder.

6.2 Continuing Representations

The representations and warranties in Article 6.1 shall be deemed repeated on each Payment Date and upon each utilization of the Facility.

ARTICLE 7: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

7.1 Events of Default

Each of the following shall constitute an Event of Default:

  • (a) Payment Default: Failure to pay any amount when due and such failure continues for more than seven (7) calendar days;
  • (b) Breach of Purpose: Use of the Facility for purposes other than those permitted under Article 2.2;
  • (c) Breach of Covenants: Breach of any material obligation under this Agreement and, where capable of remedy, failure to remedy within fourteen (14) days of notice;
  • (d) Misrepresentation: Any representation or warranty proves to have been materially incorrect when made;
  • (e) Cross-Default: Default under any other financing arrangement with the Lender or its affiliates;
  • (f) Insolvency: The Borrower becomes insolvent, enters bankruptcy, or enters into arrangements with creditors;
  • (g) Unauthorized Dealing: Any dealing with Investments in breach of Article 4.3;
  • (h) Consecutive Payment Failure: Failure to make two consecutive installment payments in full.

7.2 Acceleration and Enforcement

Upon occurrence of an Event of Default, the Lender may, by written notice to the Borrower:

  • (a) Declare the Outstanding Balance immediately due and payable;
  • (b) Cancel the Facility and prohibit further utilization;
  • (c) Direct CapiMax Platforms to:
    • (i) Suspend accrual and payment of all returns and profits on Investments;
    • (ii) Freeze withdrawal rights;
    • (iii) Liquidate Investments as necessary;
  • (d) Enforce the Pledge in accordance with Article 4.4;
  • (e) Refer the Borrower’s account to collection agencies or default databases;
  • (f) Exercise any other rights available under this Agreement or applicable law.

7.3 Calculation of Outstanding Amount

For purposes of enforcement and collection, the Outstanding Balance shall be calculated as:

Outstanding Balance = (Facility Amount + Profit Share) − Payments Received

All calculations shall be based on USD Equivalent values at the time of enforcement.

ARTICLE 8: ASSIGNMENT AND TRANSFER

8.1 Lender’s Right to Assign

The Lender shall have the absolute right, without requirement for further consent from the Borrower, to:

  • (a) Assign, transfer, novate, or otherwise dispose of all or any part of its rights and obligations under this Agreement;
  • (b) Securitize, participate, or syndicate the Facility;
  • (c) Sell or discount receivables arising under this Agreement to any third party including but not limited to:
    • (i) Financial institutions and banks;
    • (ii) Debt collection agencies;
    • (iii) Investment funds and asset managers;
    • (iv) Special purpose vehicles.

8.2 Effect of Assignment

Upon any assignment or transfer:

  • (a) The assignee shall acquire all rights of the Lender under this Agreement;
  • (b) The assignee may exercise all enforcement rights including collection, litigation, and enforcement of security;
  • (c) The Borrower shall treat the assignee as the new creditor and make all payments to or as directed by the assignee;
  • (d) The Lender shall be released from obligations to the extent assumed by the assignee.

8.3 Borrower’s Restriction

The Borrower may not assign, transfer, or otherwise dispose of any rights or obligations under this Agreement without prior written consent of the Lender.

ARTICLE 9: EARLY SETTLEMENT

9.1 Right to Early Settlement

After a minimum period of six (6) months from the Disbursement Date, the Borrower may request early settlement of the Facility by giving not less than fifteen (15) Business Days’ written notice.

9.2 Early Settlement Amount

The early settlement amount shall comprise:

  • (a) The Outstanding Balance calculated at the USD Equivalent on the settlement date;
  • (b) All accrued Profit Share up to the settlement date;
  • (c) Any early settlement fees as may be specified in Schedule A;
  • (d) All other amounts owing under this Agreement.

9.3 Release Upon Settlement

Upon receipt of the early settlement amount in full, the Lender shall:

  • (a) Issue a settlement certificate;
  • (b) Release the Pledge pursuant to Article 4.5;
  • (c) Notify CapiMax Platforms of the release;
  • (d) Permit withdrawal of Investments subject to platform procedures.

ARTICLE 10: COLLECTION AND ENFORCEMENT

10.1 Global Collection Rights

The Lender and any assignee shall have the right to:

  • (a) Engage collection agencies, legal representatives, and enforcement agents in any jurisdiction worldwide;
  • (b) Delegate authority to any office, subsidiary, affiliate, or third-party service provider to collect Outstanding Balances;
  • (c) Report defaults to credit reference agencies and financial databases globally;
  • (d) Institute legal proceedings in any competent jurisdiction.

10.2 Cross-Border Recognition

The Borrower acknowledges and agrees that:

  • (a) This Agreement may be enforced in the Borrower’s jurisdiction of residence or any other relevant jurisdiction;
  • (b) Local collection entities may be engaged to recover debts under applicable local laws while preserving the Lender’s right to calculate amounts due in USD Equivalent;
  • (c) The Borrower waives any objection to jurisdiction or venue in enforcement proceedings.

10.3 Costs of Collection

The Borrower shall be liable for all reasonable costs and expenses incurred in connection with collection and enforcement, including but not limited to:

  • (a) Legal fees on a solicitor-client basis;
  • (b) Collection agency fees and commissions;
  • (c) Court fees and administrative charges;
  • (d) Currency conversion costs.

ARTICLE 11: PLATFORM COORDINATION

11.1 Platform Communication

The Lender shall maintain communication protocols with CapiMax Platforms to:

  • (a) Record and verify Financing Instruments;
  • (b) Monitor Investment activities;
  • (c) Suspend returns upon payment default;
  • (d) Facilitate enforcement of security.

11.2 Borrower’s Authorization

The Borrower hereby irrevocably authorizes:

  • (a) CapiMax Platforms to disclose Investment information to the Lender;
  • (b) The Lender to communicate directly with CapiMax Platforms regarding the Borrower’s account;
  • (c) Implementation of restrictions on Investments upon occurrence of Event of Default.

ARTICLE 12: GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

12.1 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

12.2 Jurisdiction

The Parties irrevocably submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales and United Arab Emirates (UAE) and United States of America (USA) or any country in the world or the Borrower’s country of residence for resolution of any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

12.3 Alternative Enforcement

Notwithstanding Article 12.2, the Lender may initiate proceedings in any other jurisdiction where:

  • (a) The Borrower resides or has assets;
  • (b) Enforcement would be more effective or efficient;
  • (c) Local laws provide additional remedies.

13.1 Method of Notice

Any notice or communication required or permitted under this Agreement shall be:

  • (a) In writing in English language;
  • (b) Delivered by email, platform messaging system, registered post, or courier;
  • (c) Sent to the addresses set out in this Agreement or as subsequently notified.

13.2 Deemed Receipt

Notices shall be deemed received:

  • (a) If by email: upon transmission;
  • (b) If by platform message: upon sending;
  • (c) If by post: three (3) Business Days after posting;
  • (d) If by courier: upon delivery.

ARTICLE 14: DISBURSEMENT, ACTIVATION, AND DEBT CRYSTALLIZATION

14.1 Disbursement and Activation

Upon execution of this Agreement and issuance of the Nova Finance Instrument, Nova Digital Finance shall, directly or indirectly, transfer the value of the instrument, or any portion thereof as utilized by the Borrower, to the designated Capimax Platforms for investment purposes.

14.2 Deemed Utilization and Disbursement

The Borrower expressly acknowledges and agrees that the issuance of the Nova Finance Instrument, and/or any use thereof on Capimax Platforms, shall constitute a final and irrevocable disbursement of the financing. The instrument shall be deemed fully activated and effective immediately upon issuance and availability in the Borrower’s platform account.

14.3 Crystallization of Indebtedness

From the moment the instrument is issued and/or utilized, the Borrower’s indebtedness shall be deemed fully established, binding, and due, and repayment obligations shall commence immediately from the issuance date of the instrument, regardless of the timing or performance of investments on Capimax Platforms.

14.4 Debt Valuation in USD

The Borrower irrevocably agrees that the financing received in ProNova (PRN) shall be valued and recorded in its USD equivalent at the time of issuance of the instrument, and that all repayment obligations, debt enforcement actions, and recovery proceedings shall be calculated and claimed based on such USD value, after deducting any amounts already paid.

14.5 Payment to Investment Platforms

The Borrower acknowledges that any transfer of the instrument’s value to Capimax Platforms shall be deemed a payment made on the Borrower’s behalf, for investment purposes, and shall not affect, defer, or suspend the Borrower’s repayment obligations toward Nova Digital Finance.

14.6 Binding Effect on Capimax Platforms

Upon activation and use of the instrument, Capimax Platforms shall recognize the instrument’s value as valid and authorized financing for investment purposes, subject to their internal verification procedures, and the Borrower’s investments shall be deemed pledged and linked to this financing until full repayment or settlement.

14.7 No Conditionality

The Borrower expressly waives any right to claim that repayment obligations are conditional upon profits, returns, or performance of investments on Capimax Platforms. The debt shall remain payable in full in accordance with the agreed schedule and terms.
